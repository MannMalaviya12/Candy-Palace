My Favourite Chocolate - Mars

Mars chocolate is one of my all-time favorites. Here's some detailed information about it:

Ingredients: Mars chocolate is made with a delicious combination of Milk Chocolate (sugar, cocoa butter, chocolate, skim milk, lactose, milkfat, soy lecithin), sugar, corn syrup, hydrogenated palm kernel oil and/or palm oil, skim milk, less than 2% - milkfat, cocoa powder processed with alkali, barley malt extract, lactose, salt, egg whites, chocolate, artificial flavor. Calories: Approximately 250 calories per serving (varies by product). History: Mars chocolate has a rich history that dates back to the early 20th century. It was first introduced by Frank C. Mars, who started making and selling candy from his kitchen in Tacoma, Washington, in 1911. Founder: Mars chocolate was founded by Frank C. Mars. He played a significant role in the company's growth and success, pioneering innovations in the confectionery industry. Fun Fact: The Mars bar, one of the most popular products, was first manufactured in 1932 and has since become a classic chocolate bar loved by people around the world.
